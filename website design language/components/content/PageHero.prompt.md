Display headline + lede that opens every page; one blue accent phrase per page lives here.

```jsx
<PageHero
  title="Projects, concepts &"
  accent="other evidence."
  lede="Agents that learn, controllers that settle, small tools for large rabbit holes."
  notes={["PORTFOLIO, V1", "LIGHT ONLY", "BLOG: SOON-ISH"]}
/>
```

`notes` are optional right-aligned mono asides, auto-wrapped in parentheses. Keep the accent to a short trailing phrase.
